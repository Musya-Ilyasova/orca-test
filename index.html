FIRST COLLECTION

Simple steps into
the world of investing

Think investment is complicated and just for the experts? Think again. With Orca, anyone can start investing with ease. You can build your first collection of assets with our helpful tips and hints.

Create your first collection by discovering a range of thematic assets. Orca sorts the assets according to their fluctuation and profitability, so you can easily explore and decide what to invest in.

For those who are only starting out in investment or just want an easy way to build their portfolio from scratch, we’ve created the “First Portfolio” flow.

Orca lowers the entry threshold for users, helping them to get their first portfolio on the spot. Once they’ve set their risk level and their range of interests, they can choose from various stock collections, available to buy with one tap. These are diversified bundles of stocks from different markets, so that users can hedge risks and get started with a well-balanced portfolio of assets.
